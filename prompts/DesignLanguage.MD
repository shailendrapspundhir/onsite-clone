## Design Guidelines for OnSite 360

The design of **OnSite 360** should focus on accessibility, clarity, and ease of use, especially for blue-collar workers with varying levels of digital literacy.

### Core Design Principles

1. **Clear and Concise User Experience**
   - Simple navigation flows
   - Minimal steps to complete key actions
   - Easy-to-understand labels and prompts

2. **High-Contrast Visual Themes**
   - Clear contrasting color palettes for readability
   - Multiple theme options:
     - Light mode  
     - Dark mode  
     - Vibrant / high-visibility mode  
   - Ensures content and actions are easily distinguishable

   - AI-assisted and voice-assisted interactions
   - Support for inputs via:
     - Voice
     - Video
     - Audio
   - Real-time processing of inputs and conversion into:
     - Text
     - Form fields
     - System actions

4. **Job Description-Friendly Design**
   - Layouts optimized for explaining job requirements clearly
   - Support for structured and unstructured job descriptions
   - Easy-to-read formatting for long descriptions

5. **Voice-Assisted Search and Actions**
   - AI-powered voice search for jobs and workers
   - Voice-triggered actions such as applying to jobs or filtering results
   - Multilingual voice interaction support

6. **Consistent Design Language**
   - Rounded or rounded-rectangular UI components
   - Soft, approachable icons
   - Friendly visual language to reduce intimidation for first-time users
